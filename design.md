# DeutschForge — Design System

## Brand
German vocabulary learning app. Duolingo-inspired: bright, playful, encouraging.

## Colors
- Primary Green: `#58CC02` — main CTA, streak, success
- Green Dark: `#45A800` — pressed/active states
- Yellow XP: `#FFC800` — XP, level, coins
- Coral/Red: `#FF4B4B` — streak fire, "Again" rating, danger
- Blue: `#1CB0F6` — secondary, info, "Hard" rating
- Navy: `#1F1F1F` — text on light
- Purple: `#CE82FF` — badge accent
- Background: `#F7F7F7` — app bg
- Card: `#FFFFFF`
- Border: `#E5E5E5`

## Typography
- Font: **Nunito** (rounded, friendly)
- Display: Nunito 800 (ExtraBold) — headers, XP
- Body: Nunito 600 (SemiBold) — German words
- Body Small: Nunito 400 — definitions, metadata
- Tab labels: Nunito 700

## Component Style
- Border radius: 16px (cards), 24px (buttons), 8px (chips/tags)
- Shadows: subtle — `0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
- Buttons: full-width, 56px height, bold, rounded-full
- Card flip: 3D perspective flip animation
- Progress bars: rounded, chunky (12px height)
- Streak fire: red-orange
- XP bar: yellow gradient

## Layout
- Bottom tab bar: 4 tabs (Home, Study, Words, Profile)
- Safe area aware
- 16px horizontal padding throughout
- Generous spacing between sections (24px)

## Study Screen
- Dark background (`#1F1F1F`) for focus
- White card centered
- Large German word (32px bold)
- Flip to reveal English
- 4 rating buttons at bottom: Again (red), Hard (blue), Good (green), Easy (yellow)

## Gamification
- Streak counter with 🔥 emoji
- XP bar below streak
- Badge grid (3 cols) with earned/locked states
- Level chip in profile header
